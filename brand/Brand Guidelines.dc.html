Brand Guidelines
The system behind every tip post, story and page — so new content stays recognisably PuntMate every time.
01 · LOGO
Icon (badge) — profile pictures, favicons, small spaces
PUNTMATE
Wordmark — headers, merch, wide layouts
PUNTMATE

Clear space: leave at least the icon's own radius as breathing room on every side. Never stretch, recolour the arrow, rotate, or place on a busy photo without a scrim. Minimum size: 32px for the icon, 120px wide for the wordmark.

04 · VOICE
Short. Confident. One pick, one line of reasoning. Talk like a mate with a good tip, not a bookmaker's ad copy. Never promise a result — frame everything as value and analysis.
NZ compliance — every post carries: R18 · Gamble responsibly · 0800 654 655 · gamblinghelpline.co.nz — non-negotiable, every format, every time.

06 · ROTATION RULE
Never post the same format twice.
The feed must never show two identical-looking posts in a row. Before every post, change at least two of: look (Betslip Night ↔ Matchday Print), accent (green / blue / amber / pink), cover theme (Tip of the Week / Multi Monday / Daily Pick / Banker of the Day / Value Alert). Keep a log of the last 5 posts and don't repeat that combination. Sample weekly rotation is in README.md.
